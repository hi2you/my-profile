    NM2220 Introduction to Media Writing

    An eye-opening module that introduced me into different types of media writing, with deliverables being a new article, a feature story and social media blurbs. The skills I have picked up came in handy in my corporate communications internship where I wrote blog articles for the employee intranet.

    NM3217 Principles of Visual Communication Design

    This module taught me very important skills of graphic design and the technical knowledge of using Adobe softwares, namely Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. Previously without any design background, I have become comfortable with using the different softwares to design simple graphics, and have further ventured into video editing as well.

    NM4247 Creative Writing for the Marketplace

    A module where I unleashed all my creative juices! I have produced two short stories, a personal essay and a brand video script -- all of which felt like my babies as they were birthed from my imagination, instead of the usual theories or concepts in academic modules.

    NM4250 Data Journalism and Analysis

    The key takeaways were the use of Orange for Twitter sentitment analysis, Excel and Tableau for data visualisation, and most importantly, brushing up on my news writing skills.

    PR, Public & Corporate Comms
    NM4207 Managing Communication Campaigns

    This module assigned us to a real client and as a group, come up with a campaign that will help achieve the client's objectives. My group's campaign utilised primary research, offline publciity booths and social media content creation, and was fortunately able to achieve the client's goals at the end of the campaign.

    Data Analytics

    In CS1010S, I was exposed to Python programming, covering basic problem solving, fundamental programming constructs and variables. GET1050 is an Excel module, covering Pivot tables, VLOOKUP and macros.

    Marketing

    MKT1705X is a free elective that I took covering basic princples of marketing, including audience segmentation, 4Ps, marketing mix and pricing strategies etc. During my exchange at the University of Nottingham, I explored further by taking BUSI2045, which covered consumer behaviour, and also included statistical basket analysis, which I enjoyed learning about!

    Languages

    I have always liked learning languages, and my 923 days streak of Spanish on Duolingo is something I'm unhealthily proud of! At the same time, I wanted to not lose touch with Mandarin, hence I took up a module on Translation & Interpretation.


    Besides academic modules, I also took various online modules for further enrichment. These include Udemy's Graphic Design Essentials, Basic Web Design with HTML & CSS and User Experience Design. Even this website itself is coded personally by myself following the web design course I took! During my internship at a digital marketing agency, I wanted to dive deeper into the advertising platforms I was using - so I ended up completing Google Ads Display and Measurement Certifications :)
