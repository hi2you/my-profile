    • NM2220 Introduction to Media Writing

    An eye-opening module that introduced me into different types of media writing, with deliverables being a new article, a feature story and social media blurbs. I have learnt to identify news-worthy angles, and adapt my writing style according to the medium required. The skills I have picked up came in handy in my corporate communications internship where I wrote blog articles for the employee intranet.

    NM3217 Principles of Visual Communication Design

    This module taught me very important skills of graphic design and the technical knowledge of using Adobe softwares, namely Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. Previously without any design background, I have become comfortable with using the different softwares to design simple graphics, and have further ventured into video editing as well.

    NM4247 Creative Writing for the Marketplace

    A module where I unleashed all my creative juices! I have produced two short stories, a personal essay and a brand video script -- all of which felt like my babies as they were birthed from my imagination, instead of the usual theories or concepts in academic modules.
